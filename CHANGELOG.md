# Changelog — Just Another Wine App

## 1.0.0

Initial release.

- Multi-cellar, multi-zone wine tracking with full bottle inventory
- Wine records with producer, name, vintage, region, grapes, tasting notes
- Purchase history and consumption history per wine
- Wine label image uploads and URL imports, multi-image galleries
- External link button to Vivino, Wine-Searcher, CellarTracker, Total Wine, or Wine.com
- Wishlist with one-click "Move to Cellar" promotion into full inventory
- Ready to Drink and Currently Aging views, calculated from drinking windows
- Zone-scoped climate sensors (temperature, humidity, light, vibration, CO2, pressure)
- Zone-scoped cameras with RTSP→HLS live streaming, snapshot fallback, and snapshot history
- Unified alert engine covering sensor thresholds, sustained environmental breaches,
  rapid changes, wine drinking windows, low stock, camera/sensor offline detection,
  and system health
- Global dashboard with bottle counts, alert summary, and cellar overview
- Packaged as a native Home Assistant add-on with ingress support
